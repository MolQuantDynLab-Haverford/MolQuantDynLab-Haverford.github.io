The (Molecular Quantum Dynamics Lab (MolQuantDyn) at Haverford College, Department of Physics and Astronomy works on the development of tensor contraction methods for molecular quantum dynamics. These methods are applied to various problems, ranging from atmospheric chemistry to astrophysics, combustion and catalysis.
